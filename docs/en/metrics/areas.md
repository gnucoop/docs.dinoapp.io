# Thematic Areas

The Thematic Areas page allows you to create and manage a hierarchical list of categories for your data. This helps in organizing and filtering information across the platform.

![Areas](../imgs/metrics/areas.png)

When you open the page, you see a list of existing areas. The main columns displayed are **Area Name** and **Parent Area**. You can click on a row to select it or expand it to see more details.

## Managing Areas

### Viewing an Area
1.  In the list, locate the area you want to view.
2.  Click the **eye** (visibility) icon in the row's action menu.
3.  A dialog opens showing all the details for that area.

### Creating a New Area
1.  Click the **+** (plus) floating action button in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
    ![Areas](../imgs/metrics/areas-fab.png)
2.  A form will appear. Enter the required information, such as the **Area Name**.
3.  If this area should be a sub-category, select an existing area as the **Parent Area**.
4.  Click **Save** to create the new area.

### Editing an Area
1.  In the list, find the area you wish to edit.
2.  Click the **pencil** (edit) icon in that row's action menu.
3.  Modify the information in the form that appears.
4.  Click **Save** to apply your changes.

### Deleting an Area
1.  In the list, find the area you want to delete.
2.  Click the **trash can** (delete) icon in that row's action menu.
3.  A confirmation dialog will appear. Confirm the deletion.

!!! warning "Deleting Areas"
    Deleting an area may affect how data is categorized. Ensure no active data submissions or other metrics rely on the area before deleting it.

## Sorting and Organizing

You can sort the list alphabetically by the **Area Name** column. Click the column header to toggle between ascending and descending order.

!!! tip "Hierarchical Structure"
    Use the **Parent Area** field to create a nested, tree-like structure for your categories. This is useful for creating broad themes with specific sub-topics.
