# Checkout

The Checkout page in Dino is where you finalize your payment for services, such as upgrading your subscription plan or purchasing additional features. This page uses a secure, integrated payment system to process your transaction.

![Checkout](../../imgs/checkout/index.png)

## What You See

When you navigate to the Checkout page, you will see a secure payment form. This form is managed by Dino's payment partner and is designed to collect your payment details safely.

The page typically includes:
*   A summary of the item or subscription plan you are purchasing.
*   Fields to enter your payment information (e.g., card number, expiration date, CVC).
*   A button to complete the purchase.

## How to Complete a Purchase

Follow these steps to finalize your payment.

1.  **Review your purchase details** at the top of the checkout form.
2.  **Enter your payment information** into the secure fields provided.
3.  **Click the "Pay" or "Subscribe" button** to authorize the transaction.

!!! tip "Secure Payment"
    Your payment details are processed securely by our payment partner and are not stored directly within Dino. You will receive a confirmation email once the transaction is complete.

## After Checkout

Once your payment is successfully processed:
*   Your account will be immediately updated with the new service or subscription level.
*   You can return to the main application to begin using the new features.
*   You can view and manage your subscription from your account settings.

!!! warning "Failed Payments"
    If your payment fails, please verify your card details and ensure sufficient funds are available. Your account access will not change until a successful payment is completed.
