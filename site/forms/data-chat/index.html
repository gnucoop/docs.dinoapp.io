    Chatting with Your Data (AI)

    The Data Chat page lets you ask plain-language questions about the submissions collected for a form. The AI assistant analyses the data and returns answers in text, tables, or charts.

    Optional feature

    This feature requires a DataChat API key and may not be available in your installation. If you cannot see this option, contact your administrator.

    Accepting the Terms of Use

    The first time you use Data Chat, you will be shown a terms of use statement. Read it and click Accept to continue.

    Your acceptance is saved on your device, so you will not be asked again on future visits.

    Entering Your API Key

    After accepting the terms, you will be asked to enter your DataChat API key.

    1. Type your API key into the field.
    2. Press Enter or click the arrow button to submit it.

    The system will verify the key. If it is valid, the chat interface will load. Your key is saved on your device so you do not need to re-enter it next time.

    Invalid API Key

    If the key is rejected, check that you have entered it correctly. An error message will appear beneath the field if the key is not recognised.

    Service unavailable

    If the message "DINO-AI is not responding at the moment" appears, the service may be temporarily unavailable. Try again later.

    Asking Questions

    Once the chat is ready, you will see a preview of your data and a text input at the bottom of the screen.

    1. Click inside the text field and type your question (for example: "How many entries were submitted last month?" or "Show me a chart of responses by location").
    2. Press Enter to send.
    3. The assistant will process your question and display the answer in the chat history above. Responses may appear as text, tables, or charts.

    You can continue the conversation by asking follow-up questions. If the assistant suggests follow-up questions, you can click on them to send them directly.

    Providing Feedback

    Each response includes a thumbs-up and thumbs-down button. Use these to rate the quality of the answer. Your feedback helps improve the assistant over time.

    Troubleshooting

    "No Forms were found"

    Warning

    There are no submissions associated with this form yet. Add some entries using the submissions list before using Data Chat.

    "Sorry, you don't have enough Credits!"

    Warning

    Your account has run out of DataChat credits. A link may appear to purchase additional credits. If you need help, contact your administrator.
